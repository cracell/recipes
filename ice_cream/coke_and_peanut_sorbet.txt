Coke and Peanut Sorbet

1.5c coke, .5c peanut butter, .5c corn syrup, .25tsp vanilla extract, 1tbsp molasses, blend it up, put it in the machine, wait, add .5 cup frozen roasted peanuts right before it looks done, and chill it for a while.
