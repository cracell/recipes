10 small tomatillos
2 medium advocados
1 jalapeno (medium heat)
5 cloves of garlic
large handful of cilantro
1 lime
salt

1. Cut up garlic, tomatillos and jalapeno into large chunks and roast over medium-low on stove top for 10 - 15 minutes.

2. Allow garlic, tomatillos and jalapeno to cool down a bit.

3. Put all ingredients except lime into blender and blend until desired consistency is reached.

4. Mix in lime juice as needed and add salt to taste.

Makes 4 to 5 cups.
