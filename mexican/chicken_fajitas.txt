Chicken Fajitas

Marinade Ingredients
1 lime
1 tablespoon chopped fresh garlic
2 teaspoons olive oil, divided
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
3/4lb boneless chicken

Fajita Ingredients
3/4 lb marinated chicken cut into strips
1 green bell pepper
1 red bell pepper
1 red onion

1. Cut chicken against the grain in bite sized strips. 

2. Combine in bag with lime's juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, pepper, salt, 2 teaspoons olive oil.

3. Marinate for 1 hour - overnight

4. Preheat Oven to 400Â°

5. Grease roasting pan and add chicken from marinade.

6. Roast covered in oven for 10 minutes.

7. Cut peppers and onion into strips.

8. Add peppers and onion to chicken after the initial 10 minutes is up.

9. Cook another 10 minutes.

10. Uncover and cook another 10 minutes.

10. Serve with tortillas and/or rice.
