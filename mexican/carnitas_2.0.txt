Carnitas 2.0

2 bay leaves
1 onion
4 cloves garlic
3 pounds boneless pork butt (shoulder), rind removed, cut into 2-inch cubes
1 tablespoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon oregano
1/4 a cinnamon stick
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 - 4 dried chilis ground


1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and preheat oven to 275 degrees. 
2. Split one onion into quarters. Set aside. 
3. Season pork chunks with 1 tablespoon salt and place in a 9 by 13 glass casserole dish. The pork should fill the dish with no spaces. Split lime into quarters and squeeze juice of half over pork. Nestle squeezed lime pieces into casserole. Add 2 onion quarters, 4 cloves garlic, bay leaves, and cinnamon stick to casserole. Nestle everything into an even layer. Pour olive oil over surface. Cover dish tightly with aluminum foil and place in oven. Cook until pork is fork tender, about 3 1/2 hours.
