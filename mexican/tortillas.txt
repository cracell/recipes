Tortillas (makes 8)

2 cups all-purpose flour (250 grams)
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder (7.5 grams) (re-weigh yourself)
1 teaspoon salt (10.64grams) (re-weigh yourself)
2 teaspoons olive oil (or butter? hmm)
1 cup near boiling milk/3/4 cup near boiling water

1. Boil milk uncovered.
2. Mix together dry ingredients in bowl.
3. Add oil
4. Once mlik is just boiling, add to dry ingredients in bowl.
4. Mix with butter knife.
5. Knead until well mixed.
6. Let set for 15-20 minutes
7. Tear golf ball sized bit of dough away
8. Roll out with lots of flour on the surface and rolling pin.
9. Cook tortillas in a cast iron skillet as close to the highest heat with no oil.


Tips: Tortillas freeze well. Wrap tightly in plastic, removing any air pockets. Keeps frozen for several weeks.

Note: With water they taste like traditional tortillas. With milk they are fluffier and a bit more flavourful.
