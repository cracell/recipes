Fudge Frosting


1 1/2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate
 1/4 cup butter
 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
 1 egg white
 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1. Melt chocolate and butter in small heavy saucepan over low heat; stir to blend.
2. Beat in powdered sugar. Beat in egg white and vanilla, adding a little water, if necessary, to make smooth, slightly soft frosting
