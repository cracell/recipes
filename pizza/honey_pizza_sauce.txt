Honey Pizza Sauce

Ingredients
15 ounce can tomato sauce
1 teaspoon minced garlic (or substitute Â¼ teaspoon garlic powder)
2 tablespoons honey
3/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano or 3/4 teaspoon fresh oregano (double for classic italian taste) 
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
salt to taste (1/4 a teaspoon?)
2 tablespoons white wine (optional, if added be sure to heat sauce enough to remove the alcohol)

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Heat a little bit on the stove top for 5-10 minutes. Be sure not to burn it.
3. Let sauce sit for 30 minutes prior to use to blend flavours. 

Keeps for 3-7 days. Enough for 1 large pizza. Freezes well.
