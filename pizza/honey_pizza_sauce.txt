Honey Pizza Sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
6 fluid ounces warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C) (Equal to ¾ cup)
1 teaspoon minced garlic (or substitute ¼ teaspoon garlic powder)
2 tablespoons honey
¾ teaspoon onion powder
¼ teaspoon dried oregano (double for classic italian taste)
¼ teaspoon dried marjoram
¼  teaspoon dried basil
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
⅛ teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
salt to taste (1/4 a teaspoon?)

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Sauce should sit for 30 minutes to blend flavors; 

Keeps for 3-7 days. Enough for 1 pizza.

Based on: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/exquisite-pizza-sauce/detail.aspx
