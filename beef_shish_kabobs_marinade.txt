1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup lemon juice
10-15 leaves of fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon coarsely cracked black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 jalapenos chopped into slices
1 1/2 pounds lean beef, cut into 1-inch cubes

combine all ingredients in a plastic bag and refrigrate for 4 - 32 hours.

Take meat out of the refrigrator an half an hour before cooking to get it to room temperature.

On the grill. Sear at a high heat, then bring down to a low heat to cook through the meat. Be careful not to overcook it.
