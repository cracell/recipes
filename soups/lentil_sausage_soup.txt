Lentil Sausage Soup

Makes 8 Servings

1 cup dried lentils
8 cups chicken broth
1 cup chopped yellow onion
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 peeled and chopped carrots
1 cup finely chopped celery
8 ounces sausage
salt and pepper to taste

Put the lentils, chicken broth, onion, garlic, bay leaf, carrots and celery in a dutch oven. Simmer over low heat uncovered for 45 minutes. Add the sausage and cook for 10 minutes longer.

Season with salt and pepper. Serve with crust french bread and a crisp green salad.
