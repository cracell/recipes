Brussel Sprouts

2 tbsp butter, in cast-iron skilled; slice garlic, cook ~3 minutes, until golden, remove with slotted spoon; take sprouts, each cut in half, and arrange along the bottom of the skillet with the cut sides down, sprinkle pine nuts over them, and without moving them, cook ~15 minutes, then remove the sprouts with tongs and let the pine nuts cook another minute or two, then add the garlic, sprouts, and pine nuts all together.


Steamed with dash of salt and maybe lemon juice



Good as a side for: Steak, Pot roast
