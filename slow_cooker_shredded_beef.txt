Slow Cooker Shredded Beef


18 ounches BBQ Sauce
3 lb Chuck Roast
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons black pepper
salt to taste


Slow cooker for 8 hours. Can add onions at the end.
