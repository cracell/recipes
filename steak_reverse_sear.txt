Steak Reverse Sear

Salt several days ahead.

Add pepper right before cooking.



1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees
2. In 45 minutes - 1 hour pull out of oven when internal temperature is 130-135f
3. Rest steak for 15 minutes
4. Hard sear with cast iron for 1 minute or 2 after resting. (Dry pan or add canonla oil)
