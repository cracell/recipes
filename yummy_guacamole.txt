Ingredients
3 ripe avocados
1 lime
1 teaspoon sea salt
3 cloves of garlic
2 minced jalapenos or serrano peppers (or try a hot sauce)
1 medium red onion chopped small (but not quite minced)
# try without 1 teaspoon of olive oil
# try without 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper to taste
2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
6 cilantro leaves (minced)

Directions
Slice avocados in half and scoop avocado meat into mixing bowl. Juice lime on top and stir. Add the rest of the ingredients, stirring continuously to make the avocado soften into a thick creamy spread.
