Fried Okra

3/4 cup cornmeal
3/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup buttermilk - add 1 tablespoon vinegar to a measuring cup, fill with milk to make 1/2 cup, let sit 5 minutes
2 pounds okra, cut in 1/2 inch pieces
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
Oil for frying (I like peanut or vegetable oil)

1. Combine buttermilk and vinegar. Let sit for 5 minutes.
2. In a cast iron skillet or deep pan, heat about 6 cups of oil to 350F (125C).
3. Place cut okra in buttermilk to soak for a couple minutes while you assemble the coating.
4. In a bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, and seasonings.
5. Transfer okra to coating mixture and toss well to coat.
6. Fry okra in hot oil for about 5 minutes, stirring to fry evenly.  
7. Remove to paper towels to drain.
