Guinness Corned Beef

1 corned beef (1 - 2lbs)
1 bottle Guinness (or any dark beer)
2 cups water
1/2 head cabbage
1 package (1-2lbs? dutch/fingerling potatos)
3-4 carrots
1 onion
Any more veggies you want to add

1. Place corned beef with liquid and seasoning in deep pot.
2. Add 1 bottle Guinness and 2 cups water.
3. Bring to a boil then lower & simmer (cover with lid) for 2 1/2 - 3 hours.
4. Add potatoes (cut in halves) during the last 20 minutes.
5. Add cabbage (cut into wedges) during the last 5 minutes.
